About Quad Series

It is a Smart Energy Devices

With the increase in the tariff, monsoon fluctuations, water scarcity, every good civilian would like to optimise the Energy consumption at his residence. As expected, saving energy has equal effect as compared to putting up Solar panels and producing alternate energy. Thus one Unit of Energy saved would be more efficient and more economical than generating one unit of Energy.

The Quad Series Devices primarily provide:

  • Round-the clock Energy monitoring
  • Alerts on reaching a monthly, weekly set-point
  • Ability to control the functioning of the devices connected
  • Scheduling of ON/OFF of devices and help becoming more productive
  • Access from anywhere daily, weekly and monthly report
